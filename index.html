About Us

With the ongoing pandemic, inequalities prevelant in the world have not only become starkly visible, but it is also true to say that the crisis in exacerbating the divide. The divide can be in economic,social,political terms as well as in health care and educational sectors.Every section of the society is facing challenges in its own context.More often than not,some stories and issues go unnoticed by the masses.This project aims to highlight such stories, for ‘Vasudaiva Kutumabakam’ also implies giving a platform for all issues and all sections of the society,irrespective of his/her socio-economic status. For example:The students in rural and semi rural areas can hardly access online education;Small retailors are living on meagre earnings. A platform to voice out their stories and come up with solutions is the aim of this project. This particular project aims to research and build a knowledge base of the causative factors of these inequalities and find solutions for the same . Also, videos in the form of interviews/podcasts with subject experts will be conducted to highlight the solutions
